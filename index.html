About me

Hi, I'm Ajinkya [pronounced as Ah-gink{as in gin}-ya]. I'm a current graduate student at Indiana majoring in data science. I am a machine learning enthusiast who is making a transition to deep learning techniques from traditional supervised and unsupervised approaches. I'm a self starter and I make it a point to go learn things that fascinate me.

A little more about me
My work is centered around predictive modelling and I like spending my time working on high dimensional data separation problems. More recently I have been fascinated by deep neural networks and their ability to learn unusual functions. My current focus lies in leveraging this ability of deep learning into the problems I am trying to solve. In recent times, I have been experimenting with newer deep learning frameworks including torch by facebook. I love visualizations and I believe deeper interactions are best captured using visualization techniques.

Academically, I hold a CGPA of 3.6 and I was awarded a data science fellowship for the academic year 2016-2017 extended for the year 2017-2018. I am the current president of the Association for Information Science and Technology Chapter at Indiana University. I hold a bachelor's degree in computer science [parts of which helped me design this website] from the University of Mumbai.
