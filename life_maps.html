Faces to Places

This Memorial Day, I drove through California Highway-1, which by far is the most scenic stretch of land I've seen. I tend to travel regurlarly, I've been fortunate to visit the most amazing places in New York, Maryland, Virginia, Ohio, Indiana and California and meet a ton of wonderful people. This is a personal project I've always wanted to invest my time in. Combining my digital footprints with the some amazing pictures I managed to capture at these locations to create life long memories, something to remember the place by.
